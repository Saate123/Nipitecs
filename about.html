About NIPITECS

NIPITECS was established in 2013 through a collaboration between the Pipeline Professionals Association of Nigeria (PLAN) and Energy Corporate Africa (ECA), a renowned US-based organizer of energy events. The goal was simple yet powerful: to position Nigeria and Sub-Saharan Africa on the global map for pipeline infrastructure, technology, and security investment.


Over the years, NIPITECS has evolved into a premier platform that brings together policymakers, investors, banks, and industry professionals to share insights, build networks, and address regional pipeline challenges with practical expertise

Our Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

To advance pipeline infrastructure, technology, and security in Africa by fostering collaboration, sharing knowledge, and connecting local opportunities to global investment.

Vision Statement

To be the leading platform positioning Nigeria and Sub-Saharan Africa as a global hub for pipeline innovation, sustainable development, and energy security.
